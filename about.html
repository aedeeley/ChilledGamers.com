ChilledGamers.com was founded in November of 2014 by two online friends, PreGame and D1RTY. We started a Rust server, opened a TeamSpeak account and called ourselves ChilledGamers. Soon after we began recruiting members for a competitive Insurgency team. We've run servers all over the country for over 20 different games, including Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, DayZ, Starbound, Insurgency and Minecraft.

Today, we are just as laid back as ever! We've completed what we started this group for: build a community of laid-back and like-minded gamers to enjoy our days with. We've gathered over 50 members, and reached out to thousands of other gamers! Nowadays, you can find us playing MechWarrior Online, Squad, Hurtworld, Counter-Strike:Global Offensive and League of Legends. Our doors, online games and TeamSpeak channels are always open to be joined and enjoyed as a group.
